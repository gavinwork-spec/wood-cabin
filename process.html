Our Construction Process

From concept to completion, we ensure quality and professionalism at every step

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your needs, preferences, budget, and site requirements. Our experts work closely with you to develop the perfect cabin solution.

1-2 days Client & Design Team
2

Site Assessment & Design

Our team conducts a thorough site evaluation and creates detailed architectural designs, 3D renderings, and technical specifications tailored to your requirements.

1-2 weeks Design & Engineering
3

Proposal & Agreement

We present a detailed proposal including design plans, specifications, timeline, and transparent pricing. Upon approval, we formalize the project agreement.

3-5 days Commercial Team
4

Permitting & Approvals

We handle all necessary permits and regulatory approvals, ensuring compliance with local building codes and regulations to streamline the construction process.

2-4 weeks Regulatory Team
5

Factory Construction

Your cabin is precision-built in our controlled factory environment using premium materials and advanced construction techniques for optimal quality control.

4-8 weeks Manufacturing Team
6

Site Preparation

While your cabin is being built, our team prepares the installation site, including foundation work, utilities connection, and access preparation.

1-2 weeks Site Preparation Team
7

Delivery & Installation

Your completed cabin is transported to the site and professionally installed by our experienced installation team, ensuring perfect placement and connection.

1-3 days Installation Team
8

Final Touches & Handover

We complete all finishing touches, conduct thorough quality inspections, and provide a comprehensive handover with maintenance guidelines and warranty information.

2-3 days Quality Control Team

Quality Assurance

At every stage of construction, we maintain rigorous quality control standards to ensure your cabin meets the highest levels of excellence.
