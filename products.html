Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to common questions about our wooden cabins

What makes your wooden cabins premium quality?

Our wooden cabins are crafted from sustainably sourced premium timber, featuring advanced insulation technology, professional-grade finishes, and precision manufacturing. Each cabin undergoes 15+ quality inspections to ensure exceptional quality and durability.

How long does wooden cabin installation take?

Installation timeline varies by model: Small cabins (15-25m²) take 2-3 days, medium cabins (25-50m²) take 3-5 days, and large cabins (50m²+) take 5-7 days. This includes foundation preparation, cabin assembly, interior finishing, and final inspections.

What are your B2B partnership requirements?

We partner with established importers, distributors, and construction companies that have proven import capabilities and international trade experience. Partners must handle customs clearance, local logistics, and have adequate market knowledge. Email us at sales@woodencabin.com to discuss partnership opportunities.

What after-sales support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive after-sales support including lifetime technical assistance, maintenance guidance, and 24/7 customer service for any questions or concerns. Our dedicated support team ensures your cabin continues to perform optimally throughout its lifetime.

Can wooden cabins be customized to my specific needs?

Absolutely! We offer extensive customization options including layout modifications, material selections, color schemes, kitchen configurations, bathroom fixtures, smart home integration, and accessibility features. Our design team works closely with you to create your ideal space.

Are your wooden cabins environmentally friendly?

Yes, sustainability is core to our manufacturing process. We use FSC-certified timber, eco-friendly insulation materials, low-VOC finishes, and energy-efficient windows. Our cabins can include solar panels, rainwater harvesting systems, and green roof options for maximum environmental benefits.
